Visit Santa Fe

Enjoy culture and history in the capital city of the Province of Santa Fe.

Top three activities to do at Santa Fe

Visit historic buildings

Santa Fe has plenty of colonial style houses, as well as churches and public buildings that date back to 1649.

Drink some beer!

Beer is the most popular drink in the city, as there are factories dedicated to it's production in town.

Enjoy music shows

The city has a very diverse cultural scene, home to its own genre of music, with many concerts to pick from every weekend.
